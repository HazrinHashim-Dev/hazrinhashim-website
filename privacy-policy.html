Privacy Policy

Hazrin Hashim IFP — HazrinIFP Content Bot

This Privacy Policy describes how Hazrin Hashim IFP ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our application HazrinIFP Content Bot, which integrates with the Threads API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application via your Threads account, we may access the following information:

  • Your Threads profile information (username, user ID)
  • Content you have posted on Threads
  • Replies and comments on your Threads posts
  • Mentions of your account on Threads

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To monitor replies and comments on your Threads posts for relevant keywords
  • To generate automated reply suggestions using AI technology
  • To post replies on your behalf upon your explicit approval
  • To provide analytics and insights about your Threads engagement

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Data retrieved from the Threads API is stored securely on our private server. We retain this data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party services:

  • Meta Threads API — to access and interact with Threads content
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate AI-powered reply suggestions

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data practices.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access tokens are stored securely and never shared publicly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw authorization at any time via your Threads account settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
